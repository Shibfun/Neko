NekoToken Whitepaper - Shibarium Edition

1. Executive Summary

NekoToken (NEKO) is an ERC-20 token launched on Shibarium, a layer-2 blockchain, on March 22, 2025. With Shibarium’s 5-second block time, NEKO powers a dual-staking ecosystem where users can stake NEKO and FEED (0xe9Cb2D7ADC24Fc59FE00D6C0A0669BDF16805Fe0) to earn NEKO rewards. Capped at 1 quadrillion tokens, NEKO features a sustainable reward reduction system tailored to Shibarium’s rapid block production.

This whitepaper details the tokenomics, staking mechanics, reward structure, and the unique advantages of deploying on Shibarium.

2. Why Shibarium?

Shibarium is a layer-2 scaling solution for Ethereum, designed to offer low-cost, high-speed transactions. With a block time of just 5 seconds—compared to Ethereum’s 12 seconds—Shibarium enables faster reward distribution and transaction confirmations for NekoToken users.

  • Speed: 5-second blocks mean rewards are distributed 2.4x faster than on Ethereum.
  • Cost: Gas fees are significantly lower, making staking accessible to all users.
  • Scalability: Shibarium supports high transaction throughput, ideal for DeFi applications like NekoToken.

Impact: A 5-second block time accelerates NekoToken’s reward cycle, completing its decay in ~4.66 years versus ~11 years on Ethereum.

4. Staking Mechanics

4.1 FEED Staking

  • Amount: Fixed at 10B FEED (10,000,000,000 * 10^18).
  • Stake: stakeFeed() - One stake per address.
  • Unstake: unstakeFeed() - Returns FEED and claims rewards.
  • Claim: claimFeedRewards() - Earn NEKO without unstaking.

4.2 NEKO Staking

  • Amount: Multiples of 10,000 NEKO (10,000 * 10^18).
  • Stake: stakeNeko(amount) - Additional staking allowed.
  • Unstake: unstakeNeko() - Withdraws all NEKO and claims rewards.
  • Claim: claimNekoRewards() - Earn NEKO without unstaking.

5.2 Reward Reduction Mechanism

NekoToken’s rewards decrease linearly over time, tied to Shibarium’s 5-second block production:

  • Decay Period: 600,000 blocks (~34.7 days).
  • Max Decay: 29,400,000 blocks (~4.66 years).
  • Base Formula: Base = 50 - (Elapsed Blocks / 600,000).
  • Reward Formula: Current Reward = (Initial Reward * Base) / 50.
