1. Executive Summary

NekoToken (NEKO) is an ERC-20 token launched on Shibarium, a layer-2 blockchain, on March 22, 2025. With a 5-second block time, it powers a dual-staking ecosystem where users stake NEKO and FEED (0xe9Cb2D7ADC24Fc59FE00D6C0A0669BDF16805Fe0) to earn NEKO rewards. Capped at 1 quadrillion tokens, NEKO features a sustainable reward reduction system tailored to Shibarium’s fast pace.

This whitepaper explores its tokenomics, staking mechanics, and reward structure.

2. Token Specifications

Name: Neko
Symbol: NEKO
Decimals: 18
Max Supply: 1,000,000,000,000,000 NEKO
Blockchain: Shibarium

Leveraging Shibarium’s low fees and rapid 5-second blocks, NEKO accelerates reward distribution compared to Ethereum’s 12-second blocks.

3. Staking Mechanics

3.1 FEED Staking

  • Amount: Fixed at 10B FEED (10,000,000,000 * 10^18).
  • Stake: stakeFeed() - One per address.
  • Unstake: unstakeFeed() - Returns FEED + rewards.
  • Claim: claimFeedRewards() - NEKO without unstaking.

3.2 NEKO Staking

  • Amount: Multiples of 10,000 NEKO.
  • Stake: stakeNeko(amount) - Supports additional staking.
  • Unstake: unstakeNeko() - Withdraws all + rewards.
  • Claim: claimNekoRewards() - NEKO without unstaking.

Rewards decrease linearly over time, tied to Shibarium’s 5-second blocks:

  • Reward: Initial * Base / 50 (min at Base ≤ 1).
